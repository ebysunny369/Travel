1.  Alleppey

Alleppey, also called Alappuzha, is one of the top tourist places in Kerala. It is the best place to explore its serene backwaters encircled by coconut trees and paddy fields. Besides these, you can also enjoy a houseboat ride with your darling partner on your honeymoon visit. For this, you have to rent a Shikara. 

  • Best time to visit: August to February
  • Best places to visit: Alappuzha Beach, Revi Karunakaran Museum, Sri Krishna Temple in Ambalappuzha, Marari Beach, Krishnapuram Palace, and Punnamada Lake
  • Things to explore: Kayaking, The Snake Boat Race, sailing in houseboats, relaxing at a spa

How to reach: 

  • By Train: Alappuzha Railway station
  • By Air: Kochi International Airport
  • By Road: Cabs, Kerala Tourism Buses
