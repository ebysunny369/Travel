1.  Thiruvananthapuram

When listing the best places to visit in Kerala, Thiruvananthapuram, also called Trivandrum, must be included. The capital city of the latter southern Indian state experiences draws maximum tourist traffic from all across the world. The roadside bars, cafes, beaches, and resorts are worth to be explored here. Moreover, planning a honeymoon trip with your partner here is a good idea. 

  • Best time to visit: October to March
  • Best places to visit: Kanakakkunu Palace, Kovalam Beach, Anchuthengu Fort, Sri Padmanabhaswamy Temple, and Arruvy Waterfalls.
  • Things to explore: Fort palaces, beach fun, water sports, seafood, temples, lighthouses

How to reach: 

  • By Train: Trivandrum Station
  • By Air: Trivandrum International Airport
  • By Road: Linking main cities with national highways
