1.  Vagamon

Next on the list of top tourist places in Kerala is Vagamon. This serene, offbeat hill station will grab your attention with its misty valleys, twisty streams, tea plantations, and calm climate. If you want to experience trekking and other sports activities, then Vagamon is the ideal place to visit. Touring this bewitching valley of Kerala will make you feel so relaxed. 

  • Best time to visit: Any season
  • Best places to visit: Ulipooni Wildlife Sanctuary, Kurisumala, Pine Hill, Vagamon Meadows
  • Things to explore: Trekking, paragliding, buying local chocolates and spices, off-road hiking, and visiting spice gardens

How to reach: 

  • By Train: Kannur Railway Station
  • By Air: Kannur International Airport
  • By Road: Board state-run buses or rent a cab
