•  Varkala

    • Varkala is also among the best places in Kerala because of its splendid vistas. The business makes a perfect touring spot with its natural sceneries, holy sites, springs, and forts. Here, you can have that fantastic experience of watching the sunset and spending quality time with your loved ones. 

    • Best time to visit: October to February
    • Best places to visit: Varkala Beach, Odayam Beach, Mangrove Forest Kayaking, Edava Beach, Kappil Beach, and Ponnumthuruthu Island
    • Things to explore: Water sports, visiting temples, Kilimannor Palace, eating local cuisine

    How to reach: 

    • By Train: Trivandrum Railway station
    • By Air: Trivandrum International Airport
    • By Road: State-run buses of Kerala Tourism
